Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects like e-commerce or custom web applications can take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer project-based pricing tailored to your specific needs. Our website packages start at $2,499 for basic sites and go up based on complexity. Visit our pricing page for detailed package information.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We provide 30 days of free support after launch. We also offer affordable maintenance packages starting at $99/month that include updates, backups, security monitoring, and support.

Can you help with content and images?

Absolutely! We offer content writing services and can help source professional stock photography. We'll work with you to ensure your website has compelling, SEO-optimized content that resonates with your audience.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all our websites are built with a mobile-first approach. We ensure your site looks great and functions perfectly on all devices - smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Do you offer hosting and domain services?

Yes! We offer premium hosting optimized for performance and include 3 months free hosting with all website packages. We also provide domain registration services with privacy protection.

What technologies do you use?

We use modern, industry-standard technologies including React, Next.js, Vue.js, Node.js, and PostgreSQL. For CMS-driven sites, we work with WordPress, Strapi, or custom solutions based on your needs.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out the contact form above or give us a call. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project, understand your goals, and provide a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing.
